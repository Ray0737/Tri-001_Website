2021

The year it all began.

The Trilingual Program was first introduced in October 2021 at Srinakharinwirot University Satit Prasarnmit. It wasn't just about English, Thai, and Chinese—it was about building a community of versatile thinkers.

M.1 — M.6

Academic Path

Availability: The TRI program is available for students from M.1 through M.6.

Transition: Middle school TRI students can "walk-in" to the High School TRI Major if their grades meet the requirements.
